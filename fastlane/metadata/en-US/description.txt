DocArmor keeps your most important documents encrypted on your device — passports, insurance cards, driver's licenses, medical records, and anything else you can't afford to lose or expose.

Everything stays local. No cloud required. No account needed to get started.

ALWAYS READY WHEN YOU NEED IT
Tap once to show your passport at a hotel desk or your insurance card at urgent care. DocArmor keeps your most-used documents one tap away, organized by scenario so you're never digging through folders under pressure.

ENCRYPTED VAULT
Every document is protected by AES-256 encryption with a key that never leaves your device. DocArmor cannot read your documents. Nobody can.

HOUSEHOLD READY
Organize documents for your whole family — spouse, kids, elderly parents, even pets. Each person gets their own section. Gaps are surfaced automatically so you know who's missing what before you need it.

SMART PACKS FOR REAL SITUATIONS
Travel, medical visits, job onboarding, roadside emergencies, school enrollment, insurance claims — DocArmor groups the right documents together for each situation. Add your own custom packs for anything we missed.

RENEWAL REMINDERS
Passport expiring in 60 days. Vehicle registration due next month. DocArmor tracks expiration dates and notifies you before it's too late — for every document, for every family member.

SCAN ANYTHING
Import documents with your camera using the built-in scanner. Supports multi-page documents, both sides of cards, and automatic quality detection for blurry or glare-affected scans.

SECURE SHARING
Share a document with a trusted contact with redaction controls — front-only, back-only, or watermarked. Nothing permanent leaves your vault without your explicit choice.

EMERGENCY CARD
Opt in to a lock-screen emergency card with your blood type, allergies, and emergency contacts — visible to first responders without unlocking your vault.

SOVEREIGN CLOUD BACKUP (optional)
Katafract Sovereign subscribers can back up encrypted document metadata to their private Vaultyx vault. Image pages stay on-device. The server never sees your key.
